Blood Donation FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can donate blood?
A: Healthy individuals aged 18-65, weighing at least 50 kg, can donate blood.
Q: How often can I donate blood?
A: You can donate whole blood every 3 months (males) and every 4 months (females).
Q: Is blood donation safe?
A: Yes, blood donation is completely safe. Sterile needles are used and discarded after each donation.
Q: How long does a blood donation take?
A: The entire process takes about 30-45 minutes, with the actual donation taking around 10 minutes.
Q: What should I eat before donating blood?
A: Eat a healthy meal and drink plenty of fluids before donating. Avoid fatty foods.
Q: Can I donate blood if I have a tattoo?
A: Yes, but you must wait at least 6 months after getting a tattoo before donating.
Q: How does blood donation help?
A: Blood donation helps save lives, supports medical treatments, and benefits both the donor and recipient.
